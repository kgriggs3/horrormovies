Old > New Horror Movie Recommendations

Need horror movies to binge for the Fall season? Read the summaries of my top halloween movies below, and if one sounds interesting, click the poster to view the trailer!

#1

Fright Night (1985)
A teenager turns to his friends and a TV actor for help when he discovers his neighbor is a vampire.

#2

Beetlejuice (1988)
A deceased couple is pushed out of their house by a new family and seek help from a vengeful ghost to get rid of them.

#3

Jeepers Creepers (2001)
A brother and sister's drive home through the countryside turns dark when they encounter a flesh-eating creature.

#4

Evil Dead (2013)
Five friends take a trip to a cabin in the woods but unintentionally summon demons when they discover a book.

#5

As Above So Below (2014)
A team of explorers go into the Paris catacombs searching for treasure, but discover dark secrets.

#6

Autopsy of Jane Doe (2016)
A father and son's typical coroner job takes a dark turn as they try to identify the body of a young woman.

#7

Gonjiam: Haunted Asylum (2018)
A team for a horror web series explores an abandoned asylum to determine if ghosts are real and quickly get an answer.

#8

Barbarian (2022)
A woman stays at an Airbnb and soon realizes the house is not as normal as it originally seemed.
